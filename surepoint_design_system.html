Design Philosophy

Modern Financial Services Aesthetic

This redesign brings SurePoint Equity into 2025 with a bold, confident visual identity that communicates trust, speed, and innovation. The color palette combines professional navy tones with energetic orange and teal accents, creating a distinctive brand presence in the real estate lending space.

Key Design Decisions:

  • Typography: Syne for headlines (modern, bold) + IBM Plex Sans for body (professional, readable)
  • Mobile First: Optimized for phone viewing, scales beautifully to desktop
  • Gradient Accents: Subtle gradients add depth without overwhelming
  • Generous Spacing: Breathing room enhances readability and professionalism
  • Micro-interactions: Hover states and transitions create engaging experience
  • Clear CTAs: Orange primary buttons demand attention and drive conversions
